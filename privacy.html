TL;DR — We respect your privacy. LuckyVerse.tech is a personal portfolio. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Most of our tools are client-side only, meaning your data never leaves your browser.

LuckyVerse.tech is the personal website and portfolio of Lucky, a cybersecurity student and developer based in Surat, India. This policy covers all pages and subdomains under luckyverse.tech, including CodeArena, Jalsa Bank, SphereWalk, SoundLux, and any future projects.

This is a non-commercial personal portfolio. We are not a company and do not operate for profit.

02 Data Collection & Usage

🏦 Jalsa Bank (Financial Tool)

Strict Privacy Commitment: Jalsa Bank is a 100% client-side application. All financial inputs — amounts, interest rates, member names — are processed locally within your device's browser using JavaScript.

WE DO NOT STORE, TRANSMIT, OR ACCESS YOUR FINANCIAL DATA. Nothing you enter is ever sent to any server. If you clear your browser cache, any locally saved data may be lost permanently.

🎧 SoundLux (E-Commerce Platform)

SoundLux uses Supabase for authentication and product management. When you create an account, Supabase may store your email address and encrypted password in accordance with Supabase's Privacy Policy.

We do not store payment information. Any financial transactions would be handled by third-party processors.

🛡️ CTF Challenges

For educational hacking challenges (CTFs), we may temporarily collect:

  • Usernames or aliases (not your real name)
  • Challenge scores and completion times for leaderboards
⚠️
Security Warning: Never submit real passwords, personal credentials, or sensitive data into any CTF input field.

03 Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to host and deliver our content. These services have their own privacy policies and may independently collect technical data such as IP addresses and browser type:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our static website files. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Cloudflare: Provides DDoS protection, DNS management, and CDN services. Cloudflare may set a strictly necessary security cookie (__cf_bm) to distinguish humans from automated bots. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Used for deploying some sub-projects. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Backend-as-a-service for specific projects. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

04 Cookies

The core LuckyVerse.tech portfolio site does not use tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or behavioural tracking software on this site.

Our third-party providers (Cloudflare, Supabase) may use strictly necessary technical cookies as outlined above. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
